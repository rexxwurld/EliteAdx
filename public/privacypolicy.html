Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

At EliteAdx, your privacy matters to us. We aim to be transparent about the data we collect, why we collect it, and how it helps improve your experience. This policy is friendly, straightforward, and designed to match the style of our Terms of Service.

1. What Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide our advertising and marketing services effectively. This includes:

  • Anonymous device information (like browser type, OS, and screen size)
  • Location info derived from IP address (never precise GPS without permission)
  • Interaction with ads to track conversions and improve campaigns

2. Cookies & Identifiers

We use cookies and anonymous identifiers to help deliver relevant ads and measure performance. They are:

  • Non-personally identifiable
  • Used only for ad targeting and analytics
  • Removable by adjusting your browser settings

3. How We Use Your Information

The collected data helps us:

  • Show ads that are more relevant to your interests
  • Track ad performance for advertisers
  • Improve the EliteAdx platform experience

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. Only authorized personnel can access information needed to run our services.

5. Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. Any sharing with third-party advertisers is strictly for campaign purposes and remains anonymous.

6. Your Rights

You can manage cookies or opt out of interest-based advertising. Contact us anytime if you have questions about your data or privacy.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our platform means you accept any changes.
